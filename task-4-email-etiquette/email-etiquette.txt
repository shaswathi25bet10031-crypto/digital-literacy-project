Do’s 
1. Curate your profile like a portfolio – Share projects, achievements, and skills.
2.Follow people who inspire growth – Mentors, tech creators, researchers, etc.
3.Pause before reacting – Take a moment before commenting or posting emotionally.
4.Use it to network, not just scroll – Connect with seniors, alumni, and professionals.
5.Audit your profile regularly – Remove posts that don’t represent who you are now.


Don’ts 
1.Don’t make social media a diary – It’s not a good idea to share personal struggles.
2.Don’t argue to win – It ends up damaging your reputation.
3.Don’t follow every trend blindly – Some trends might not be safe for you.
4.Don’t forget about your digital footprint – Employers might check it out.
5.Don’t live life through comparisons – What you see is not real.
