Title: Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students

1. Do not download software or apps from unknown websites.
2. Regularly upgrade your operating system, antivirus software, and applications.
3. Set up automatic backups of your important files to external hard drives or cloud storage.
4. Do not click on suspicious links or open suspicious emails.
5. Use strong passwords and change them regularly.
6. Enable Two Factor Authentication (2FA) for all critical accounts.

 Financial / UPI Safety Tips:
7. Do not share OTPs, UPI PINs, or bank details with anyone under any circumstances, even if they claim to be from a bank.
8. Always check UPI payment requests and IDs before making a payment.
